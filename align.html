Contract  Overview

This engagement with Align Technology, creators of InvisAlign, consisted of two separate contracts. First, an 8-week contract leading a design team to support doctors through COVID-19. Second, a 4-week contract to develop a design process for the company and create curriculum to educate the employees.

Doctors  Support  During  COVID-19

Role + Team

I led a global team of 17 diverse employees, most of whom were unfamiliar with the design process. We started with a broad question, and I led us through to low-fidelity testing.

Challenge

Align wanted to better its relationship with its doctors through piloting two to three solutions.

Outcome

We tested 5 concepts and handed off 3 for piloting. Soon, these concepts or versions of them can help Align's doctors to better bounce back through and after COVID-19.

Overview

COVID-19 and Dentistry/Orthodontics

In 2020, COVID-19 emerged and began affecting most people in the world. Most dentists and orthodontists have faced immense stress this year as they had to close their offices for months and consider the future of their practices. Leadership experience

As the subteams' learnings in "Concepts with results" detail, we moved very quickly, and with agility. With a short amount of time and a team of novice designers used to the slow pace of the old corporation, we hard to push hard. I enjoyed the experience, especially teaching people we can achieve what they may think is impossible.

Company  Design  Process

Role + Team

This was independent with support and direction from Align's Director of Culture Transformation.

Challenge

Align Technology is getting to be an older company, locked in some old, slower ways of moving. Align wanted to become more agile and modern and decided to start with its problem solving techniques. They brought me in to create a new, very customized process for Align to approach problems.

Outcome

The Director of Culture Transformation loved the Instructor Deck I put together and the process as a whole. Throughout, she was already using some of my resources and was super excited to bring the process to scale.

Overview

Brief Process Overview

To develop the Align-specific design process, I had to be familiar with Align and its customers. Luckily, I had just finished my second user-centered project with the company and have learned a lot about their doctors and their internal processes. After gathering as much content I thought would be related as possible from my own previous studies to new research, I started considering what was most important for understanding these doctors and making Align a more user-centered company. Throughout the process, I spoke with the Director of Culture Transformation who would be teaching this to the employees. Using my experience with Align employees and design, I was able to create a fully customized process the director loved and started using before it was even finished.
